Obituary Collection

Obituaries were collected from various funeral homes in Milwaukee and the Milwaukee area. We collected obituaries from the past 5 years only (2021-2025) and employed stratified random sampling to ensure an equal number of obituaries were collected foreach year. For each obituary, we observed various features including names, dates of birth, dates of death, ages, genders, word counts, and what metaphors were present.


Data Analysis

The results of these observations were recorded In an excel spreadsheet where we performed descriptive statistics to understand in what proportions the various metaphors occur.
