Initial Collection

  • As of now, we have collected a total of 180 obituaries with 36 from each year.

  • Of the obituaries, 95 belong to men and 85 belong to women.

  • The average age at the time of death is 71. The oldest age recorded was 101 and the youngest was 26.

  • The average word count of the obituaries was 239.6 with the highest word count being 952 and the lowest word count being 13.

  • In total, we recorded 266 references to death (either metaphoric or literal) and 4 obituaries where no mention of death is made at all for a total of 270 references (or lack thereof) to death.
